Scripture Reading: Rom. 5:1-11

I. We are justified by faith (v. 1)
   A. This is by grace through faith
   B. Not by works (Eph. 2:8-9)

II. We have peace with God (vv. 2-5)
   A. Access to grace through faith
   B. Hope in the glory of God

III. God demonstrates His love (vv. 6-8)
   A. Christ died for the ungodly
   B. While we were still sinners

IV. Much more shall we be saved (vv. 9-11)
   A. Saved from wrath through Him
   B. Reconciled to God through His death
